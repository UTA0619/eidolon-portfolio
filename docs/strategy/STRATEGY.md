# Eidolon — Pivot Strategy: from RPG to Twin (Identity OS)

> Status: Draft v0.1 · 2026-06-14 · Owner: Founder
>
> This document reframes Eidolon around the thesis that survived an unsentimental
> review: the unicorn is **not "an AI RPG."** It is a **persistent personal AI — a
> Twin — that learns who you are and, over time, acts on your behalf.** The game is
> the *wedge*, not the product. This strategy sequences the wedge → the OS, and sets
> the business model the monetization redesign (issue #121 + tiers) must implement.

---

## 1. The one-line thesis

**Everyone will have a personal AI. The winner is not the smartest model — those
commoditize — it is the one that knows you best and that you trust most. Eidolon earns
both: a daily game that is actually fun accumulates a private model of you that no one
can replicate, governed by a constitution that makes the Twin unmistakably yours.**

We are building the **identity layer for the agentic era**, disguised as a game you
look forward to every morning.

## 2. Why the RPG framing caps the company

| Framed as a game | Framed as a Twin |
|---|---|
| Competes with idle RPGs on content volume | Competes on how well it knows you |
| Moat = dungeon variety (commodity, LLM-cloneable) | Moat = your private personality+memory+reality graph (un-cloneable) |
| Monetize power (kills D3) or cosmetics (low ARPU) | Monetize relationship, cognition, agency (high LTV, D3-safe) |
| Valuation comp: mobile game studio (~3–5× revenue) | Valuation comp: personal-AI / identity platform (>$100M on narrative + retention) |

The game is the best part of the funnel, not the ceiling. Keep it. Stop letting it
define the company.

## 3. The wedge → OS sequence (3 acts)

**Act 1 — WEDGE (now → 12mo): the game harvests the bond.**
The soul-twin RPG is low-stigma (vs. "AI girlfriend"), fun, and a daily ritual. Its real
job: get people to feed the Twin personality signals, episodic memories, and reality data
(steps/sleep/HRV/mood) — willingly, daily, for years.
- North-star: **Twin Depth × Retained Users** (not MAU). See §6.
- Win condition: D30 retention and *data depth per user* climbing together.

**Act 2 — BRIDGE (6–18mo): the Twin steps out of the dungeon.**
The Twin starts reflecting the user's *real* life back: "you've slept badly four nights —
your Eidolon is restless too. Want to talk about it?" Journaling, mood, habit insight, a
weekly "what I noticed about you" reflection. The product becomes a mirror and companion,
not just a game.
- Win condition: a rising share of sessions are Twin-conversation, not combat.

**Act 3 — OS (18mo+): the Twin acts.**
Agentic and consented: it drafts messages in your voice, remembers your relationships,
prepares your day, eventually represents you in low-stakes negotiations (per the
governance ONTOLOGY's agency vector — bounded, reversible, waking-consent for stakes).
This is the ECHO/PARALLEL identity-OS vision. This is where the >$100M valuation lives.
- Win condition: agent actions taken on the user's behalf per week.

Each act funds and de-risks the next. We never bet the company on Act 3 before Act 1's
retention is proven.

## 4. Who it's for (ICP) and where we start

**Beachhead:** Japan first. Rationale: the team and i18n are JP-native; AI-companion
behavior already has cultural traction in Japan; APP I/privacy expectations align with our
governance-first posture; less crowded than the US Replika/Character.AI fight. Expand to
EN once Act-1 retention is proven.

**Emotional ICP (who it *刺さる*-s hardest):** 18–30, mobile-first, AI-curious, on a
self-improvement arc, who want to be *seen* and to *grow* — the Character.AI/Replika
emotional segment, reached through a **game** (lower stigma) with a **self-improvement
spine** (more durable than romance roleplay).

## 5. Moat (why this compounds and can't be copied)

1. **The you-graph.** Personality (Big Five) × episodic memory × reality timeline,
   accumulated daily. Switching cost rises every day the Twin remembers more. A competitor
   starting today cannot replay your last 400 mornings.
2. **Trust as brand.** In an era of creepy, extractive AI, "the AI that is *constitutionally*
   yours" (the Doctrines: you own it, it can't be sold, real deletion, no power-for-pay) is a
   genuine differentiator — especially in JP/EU. Governance is not overhead; it is positioning.
3. **Habit + reality coupling.** The real-world data loop (steps/sleep/HRV) ties the Twin to
   the user's actual life — a Pokémon-GO-class hook the company has barely used.

## 6. North-star & business model (sets up monetization redesign)

**North-star metric:** **Twin Depth × Retained Users.** Twin Depth = a composite of memory
richness, days of reality data, and personality confidence. Revenue follows depth, not MAU.

**Monetize the relationship, never power (D3):**
- **Free** — the game + a basic Twin (cheaper model, bounded memory).
- **Bond / Premium** — the "deeper relationship" tier: longer memory horizon, better
  cognition (Sonnet), richer weekly reflections, voice, personality & appearance
  customization, exports/keepsakes (year-in-review, printable Twin chronicle). This tier
  *aligns COGS with payers* — premium pays for the expensive model, fixing the unit-economics
  hole (§ review finding).
- **Cosmetic / story gacha (D3-safe)** — identity expression and lore, never advantage. The
  #121 catalog rewrite implements this.
- **Future (Act 3)** — agent actions: transactional and/or B2B2C.

This is the model issue #121 + the tier redesign (next step, B) will encode in code.

## 7. The investor narrative (one paragraph)

"Personal AI is inevitable; the model layer is commoditizing. The durable moat is the
*relationship* — the private model of a person and the trust to act for them. Eidolon
acquires that relationship through a game people genuinely enjoy daily, accumulates a
you-graph no competitor can replay, and governs it with a constitution that makes the Twin
provably the user's own. We start as the most-loved AI companion in Japan and become the
identity layer for the agentic era." TAM: personal AI + digital identity — measured in
hundreds of millions of people.

## 8. Honest risks & counters

- **Replika / Character.AI exist.** Counter: stigma-free game wedge, self-improvement spine,
  governance/trust as brand, and the reality-data loop they lack.
- **LLM commoditization.** Counter: the moat is the you-graph + trust, not the model; we treat
  models as swappable (the fallback chain already does).
- **Pivot kills the fun.** Counter: Act 1 keeps the game central; the Twin narrative layers in,
  it does not replace the loop.
- **Ethical monetization → thin revenue.** Counter: §6 monetizes depth/cognition/agency, whose
  willingness-to-pay rises with attachment — the opposite of a cosmetic-only ceiling.

3. **North-star switches from MAU to Twin Depth × Retained Users.**
